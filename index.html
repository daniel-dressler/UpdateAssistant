About

Keeping Wordpress updated is essential. Letting Wordpress become outdated is a sure-fire way to get your websited hacked. Updating Wordpress itself is an easy process but can become troublesome for webmasters with many websites.

UpdateAssistent makes it effortless to keep your Wordpress installs up to date. Once your websites have been added to UpdateAssitent checking that they are all updated is as simple as visiting this page. Since we link directly to the Wordpress Update Panel updating itself is even easier with UpdateAssistent.

								What does a 「?」 version mean?
								A 「?」 in the version column in general means a failure to detect the wordpress version. 
								This can be caused by there simply not being a wordpress installation at the url. 
								Or that the wordpress isntall is not reporting its version with the standard meta tag. 
								Some admins turn off the version reporting for "security" and some themes alter the tag.
							

							
							

								Why might a website be 「MAYBE」 updated?
								A 「MAYBE」 updated status is caused either by an unknown version or a bug. 
								If the version is properly reported yet the 「Updated?」 status is 「MAYBE」 please report this as a bug.
							

							
							

								Does the WordPress version meta tag pose a security risk?
								I am perhaps the most paraoinoid person I know when it some to security. With that said I do not suggest disabling the version meta tag.
								

 Botnets and crackers will attack your wordpress with automated scripts regardless of what version it is. 
								Removing the version tag does not change anything. 
								If your wordpress is outdated you will get hacked.
								


								So no, the version meta tag does not pose a security risk.
